Facilities and Features

State-of-the-art facilities designed for active, hands-on learning

Our campus offers state-of-the-art facilities and a nurturing environment designed for active, hands-on learning. We maintain large, safe classrooms and outdoor play areas that encourage exploration. Classrooms are equipped with age-appropriate learning materials and technology to support our curriculum.

Play-Based Learning Environment

We embrace a play-based learning environment inspired by Montessori and Reggio Emilia philosophies. This means children learn through doing: they explore concepts at their own pace, guided by teachers who facilitate discovery and creativity.

Workstation-Based Learning

Dedicated learning stations for different subjects that allow students to engage in real-life, hands-on learning every day.
